Leg Ball Movement Demonstration

About

This is a simple demonstration of the vastly improved movement system in Leg Ball.

Instructions

Use WASD to move, hold down the Left Mouse Button (LMB) to aim yourself and release LMB to launch yourself. Press F to add a single leg to the ball, press Shift+F to add ten legs to the ball. Please note that the player's ability to manually add legs to the ball will not be supported in the final game. This is purely for testing purposes.
